The Casio F91W is one of the most iconic digital watches ever made. First introduced in 1989, it has become a global classic, known for its simplicity, reliability, and affordability. The F91W features a lightweight resin case, a digital display, a stopwatch, an alarm, and an LED backlight. Despite its simple design, it has a cult following, thanks to its retro appeal and timeless design. It's water-resistant and offers a long battery life, making it a favorite for everyday wear.
